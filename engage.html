Work With Pushpendra

Engage Pushpendra

Each engagement is taken on selectively. Write to us with what you have in mind.

01

Production House & Cast Workshops

For filmmakers, directors & OTT productions

Pushpendra works in pre-production and on set, bringing actors to a place of deep character readiness, building shared performance language and reducing the time and cost of getting there on the floor.

02

Private Coaching for Executives

One executive. One process. Complete confidence.

Private, confidential coaching for leaders who want to work on how they show up under pressure. The process is designed from scratch around you: your role, your specific challenges, your next moment that matters. Taken on selectively.

03

Private Coaching for Actors

One actor. One process. Built entirely around you.

Whether you are beginning your journey or preparing for a specific role, private coaching is available on a project basis — confidential, one-on-one, designed from scratch around where you are and what you need.

04

Team Workshops for Organisations

Presence that earns respect. For the whole room.

From half-day sessions to multi-day programmes — built around what your team specifically needs, not a standard curriculum. Most effective in groups of up to 25, where real engagement with every person is possible.

05

The 4-Week Intensive

For a small group of committed actors

A structured 4-week intensive for 10 to 20 actors — five days a week — building from somatic awareness and truthful behaviour through character discovery to full scene performance. Forms when the right group is ready.

06

Acting for Directors & Screenplay Writers

Understanding performance from the inside

Participants experience the fundamentals of acting by doing, beginning with acting styles across film history and culminating in a short scene directed by each participant on their mobile.

07

Embodied Transformation

Somatic awareness & contemplative practice

Rooted in stillness, somatic awareness and contemplative practice. Not technique. Not performance. A deeper encounter with the self. Open to all — actors, leaders, writers and anyone drawn to the practice of presence.

08

Children & Young Performers

Screen training for young actors

Acting training for children built specifically for screen — films, OTT and other image media. Taken on selectively, in collaboration with filmmakers, production houses, theatre groups and educators. Safe, nurturing and deeply professional.

09

Custom Engagements

Something else entirely

Have a specific need that doesn't fit the above? Pushpendra has designed bespoke workshops for universities, theatre companies, cultural institutions and corporate teams with unusual briefs. Write to us with what you have in mind.

Every engagement begins with a conversation. Tell us what you're working on — we'll tell you honestly whether and how we can help.
